❓ “What is the USE CASE of your project?”

“The main use case of my project is to provide a secure and simple digital voting platform where users can register, authenticate, and cast their vote without duplication. It can be used in small-scale elections like college elections, organizational voting, or surveys where fairness and unique participation are required.”


Can it be used in real government elections?”

“Currently, it is a basic academic model. For real-world elections, it would require strong security measures like encryption, biometric verification, and server-level deployment.”
